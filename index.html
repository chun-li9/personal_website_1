About me

Some interesting facts about me

My name is Chun, I was born in China and came to Montreal when I was in secondary 4. Currently, I’m a U2 student at McGill University studying software engineering. I entered university in 2020 with interest in Artificial Intelligence and my plan is to take AI as a minor program starting from U3.

Through the several academic projects I did in school (which are introduced in the projects section on this page), I discovered my passion in backend and frontend development. I’m especially interested in frontend development, as designing user interface brings me a lot of fun.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing the violin, travelling with my friends and photographing. Sometimes I also do some small projects to practice my skills and learn more new things as I believe in learning by doing!
